As a faculty or staff member of an educational institution, you are expressing interest in applying for Unity-specific training and resources sponsored by Louisiana Economic Development in partnership with Digital Twin Studios.

This program is designed to provide educators with Unity Certified Instructor training and software integrating in-demand skills into institutional curricula.

Phase 1 begins with assessing institutions and their educators to identify:

  1. Current levels of exposure and investment in real-time 3D content development technology competency.
  2. Factors that positively impact diversity, equity and inclusion levels in content development technology.
  3. Plans to expand outreach efforts to increase interest and resource investment in content creation technology.
